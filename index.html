WHAT IS ICE HOCKEY?

Ice hockey is a winter team sport played on ice skates, usually on a rink. It consists of two opposing teams, composed of six skaters each. The players use hooked sticks to drive a small rubber disk into the other team's goal; the winner is the team to score most goals. In Canada, the United States, and some European countries the sport is known simply as hockey in everyday language.
