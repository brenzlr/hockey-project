History of Ice Hockey

The origin of ice hockey is unknown; however, ice hockey probably evolved from the game of field hockey has been played in Northern Europe for centuries. Discovering early sticks and ball games, is evidence that such a game has been played in ancient Egypt and Greece. According to the International Ice Hockey Federation (IIHF), the first organized ice hockey game was played on March 1875. Since then, it has been always changing.
